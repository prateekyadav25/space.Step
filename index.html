About space.step

Pushing the boundaries of rocket engineering

At space.step, we are dedicated to advancing the frontiers of space exploration through innovative rocket engineering solutions. Our team of expert engineers and scientists work tirelessly to develop cutting-edge propulsion systems, spacecraft designs, and launch technologies that make space more accessible.

With over 15 years of experience in the aerospace industry, we have successfully delivered projects ranging from small satellite launchers to heavy-lift launch vehicles. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has earned us recognition as a leading force in rocket engineering.
